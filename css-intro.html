Focaccia with Caramelised Onion, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your tastebuds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups
  • instant yeast
  • salt
  • lukewarm water
  • evo oil

For the topping

  • yellow onions
  • blsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • brie
  • cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
